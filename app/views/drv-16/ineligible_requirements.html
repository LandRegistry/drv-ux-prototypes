Use the existing Land Registry service to get full title documents or an indication of whether a property is at risk of flooding.

The guide to getting information about property and land will help you work out which documents you need.

You can only get a summary of the property register from this trial service. The summary just contains:

  • property address and title number
  • current owner’s name and address
  • what they paid for the property
  • whether the property is freehold or leasehold (known as ‘tenure’)
  • the length of the lease if it’s leasehold
  • if there is a mortgage on the property, the lender’s name and address

You’ll also be able to get full title documents and flood risk information here in future.
