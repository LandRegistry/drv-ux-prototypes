Order an ‘official copy’ of the register

To get copies to use as proof of ownership, eg in a court case or for a visa application, you need to order official copies.

Download and fill in an application for official copies of documents and send it to Land Registry with your fee.

Citizen Centre
PO Box 6350
Coventry
CV3 9LP

Your official copies will arrive in less than a week.

Fees

Document Fee
Title register (official copy) £7
Title plan (official copy) £7
