  • Version 18 Work in progress (Public beta)

    Version 18 is Public Beta first drop.

    The start journey from 17, and the rest from v16/15. with tweaks to copy - explanations and references to other services

    Unusual Title types (google doc)

    • in (address notes): foreshores, airspace, advertising hoardings, gable end walls
    • out: mines and minerals, caution against first registration, Profit a Prendre, manorial rights, rent charges
    Changelog

    Version 17 taught us

    Research findings newsletter

    • Expected

      Everyone clicks the Start Now button, even when asked/shown picture to use beta.

    • Success

      What information are you looking for (Page2): worked well -> wouldn't fail people with the wrong route to answer their query

    • Observed

      Page 2: People didn't open the links which explain what they will get

    • Hypothesis

      Page 2: People will choose the route which sounds best like the task set (how it's phrased).

    • Hypothesis

      Page 2: Route choice - people associate land with needing a map or plan (and probably addresses with property (property = building)

    • Success

      sign-in page - people understand this page now, and that they need an account, and how to get one

    • Observed

      sign-in page - half the people don;t understand why/complain about needing an account

    • Observed

      people expect to get search after the start page / route page. not a sign-in page

    • Learned

      accordian links on search results and confirm page - 'the link is the thing' does not work. When changed to make the link a question like 'what is caution?' or 'why not?' people open them

    • Expected

      once signed-in everyone proceed quickly to conform page - then they stop and read. and the read and understand the page - and what will happen next.

    • Expected

      On the summary page - everyone wants to take this information away / save it.

    • Interesting

      This time no one mentioned "private information" will be revealed. Perhaps because the tasks set were different (not about 'imagine about your house')

    • Success

      people find the summary info / presentation of the summary info, better than the text in the pdf

    Research history: Sprint 28

    Changelog

    Version 16 taught us

    • Expected

      Everyone clicks the Start Now button, even when asked to use beta.

    • Expected

      people don't read the information on the start page

    • Problem

      still shocked when sign-in page comes straight after start (as start says, 'search for property information')

    • Problem

      sign-in page - most people won't proceed on their own - barrier to 'succeed first time'

    • Expected

      search and navigating results still work really well

    • Success

      Confirm your order - seems to have removed lingering tendency to confuse some people with summary page

    • Success

      Confirm your order - people stop, read, & understand what they will get

    • Finding

      Confirm your order - people gravitate to map, and try to interact with it. This might be contributing to people lingering on this page

    • Alert

      Confirm your order page - people shocked that "private information" will be revealed in summary

    • Alert

      Summary page - people shocked that "private information" is revealed (same every time we test)

    • Improved

      cancellation policy wording better; 'contract' confuses (all recognise that you have to accept these things to continue - don't really care what it says)

    • Finding

      'realistic' worldpay screen caused no problems.

    • Finding

      worldpay 'sigh'. but familiar

    • Finding

      worldpay confirmation - people expect a confirmation of payment - by email

    • Confirmed

      People want confirmation of things & to keep things - so write down, screenshot, copy and paste information like: payment confirmation text, summary information
